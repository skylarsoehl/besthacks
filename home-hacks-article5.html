Vacuum Seal 1oz of Stale Granola Today

You know you want your 1 oz bags of stale granola to be preserved forever so it can sit on a shelf and grow mold. Don't use a regular plastic bag. Use aluminum foil and iron it for 1 hour to preserve your precious granola!

With your hand, hold the bag closed by the top.

Insert the vacuum cleaner (I removed all the tube attachments to make it easier to insert) and turn it on until the bag stops shrinking.

...And bask in the glory of all the space you saved in your cabinet! Just make sure the bag has no holes and the knot leaves the bag completely sealed.

Then, wrap it up in aluminum foil to preserve its goodness and iron it for an hour to keep it good and fresh forever.

When the time comes to restore all your stuff to its air-filled glory, just rip the foil, open the bag and watch your granola spring back to life!
